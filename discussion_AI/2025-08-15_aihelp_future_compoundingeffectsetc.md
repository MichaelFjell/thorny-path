2. **Central Fatigue:** You’ll notice more systemic fatigue, higher resting HR, maybe sleep quality drops if you’re pushing up distance/pace too fast without rest/deload weeks.
3. **Motivation and Psychological Fatigue:** At first, progress is dopamine-rich. Once the “hard” runs become longer and more mentally taxing, motivation takes a hit.

- You feel **more joint/tendon pain** or “niggles” that linger between runs.
